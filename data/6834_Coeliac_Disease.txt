<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Coeliac disease(CD) is an autoimmune inflammatory condition affecting the small intestine. CD is caused by an adverse reaction to the protein found in the grains called gluten.  Gluten is found in wheat, barley, rye, and beverages such as beer.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Fatigue</li><li>Diarrhea</li><li>Abdominal pain</li><li>Bloating and flatulence</li><li>Indigestion</li><li>Constipation</li><li>Unexpected weight loss</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
