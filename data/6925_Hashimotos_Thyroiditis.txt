<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Hashimoto’s thyroiditis is an autoimmune thyroid condition involving inflammation or swelling of the thyroid gland. It may be caused by an abnormally high or low level of thyroid hormones in the body.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Fatigue</li><li>Weight gain</li><li>Constipation</li><li>Dry skin</li><li>Depression</li><li>Palpitations</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
