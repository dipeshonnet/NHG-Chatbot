<p>Stress is managed by the stress hormone, cortisol released from the adrenal glands. Adrenal dysfunction is associated with an imbalance of cortisol and DHEA which leads to stress and fatigue.  Stress impacts the body mentally and physically and may be a contributing factor in complex chronic conditions.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
