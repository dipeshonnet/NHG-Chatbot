<p>Gout is an arthritic condition associated with an accumulation of uric acid crystals in and around the joints. It causes sudden<br>attacks of pain and swelling and may be painful and debilitating.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Severe pain in one or more joints</li><li>Hot and tender joint</li><li>Swelling in and around the affected joint</li><li>Redness and inflammation</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
