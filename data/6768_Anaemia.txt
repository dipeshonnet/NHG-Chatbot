<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Anemia is a condition that develops when your blood lacks enough healthy red blood cells or hemoglobin. Hemoglobin is<br>the main part of red blood cells and binds oxygen. If you have too few or abnormal red blood cells, or your hemoglobin is<br>abnormal or low, the cells in your body will not get enough oxygen.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Lethargy</li><li>Shortness of breath</li><li>Palpitations</li><li>Pale complexion</li><li>Headache</li><li>Hair loss</li><li>Difficulty swallowing</li><li>Altered sense of taste</li></ul>
