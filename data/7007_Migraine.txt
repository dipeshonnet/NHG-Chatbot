<p>Migraines are moderate or severe headaches felt as a throbbing pain on one side of the head. Migraines can be associated with auras, ashing lights, or no auras.  Migraines can be caused by hormonal imbalance, stress, fatigue, or certain dietary changes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Nausea</li><li>Vomiting</li><li>Increase sensitivity to light and sound</li><li>Concentration abdominal pain</li><li>Diarrhea</li><li>Headache numbness or tingling</li></ul>
