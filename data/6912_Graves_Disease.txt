<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Graves’ disease is an autoimmune thyroid disease when the thyroid gland produces too many Thyroid hormones and becomes overactive. It affects middle-aged women and may be genetic.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Irregular heartbeat</li><li>Nervousness</li><li>Anxiety</li><li>Hyperactivity</li><li>Mood swings</li><li>Difficulty sleeping</li><li>Muscle weakness</li><li>Fatigue</li><li>Persistent thirst</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
