<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Infertility is associated with an inability to conceive which may be due to an imbalance of hormones both in the male and female. Common causes of infertility may include a lack of regular ovulation, blockage of fallopian tubes, endometriosis, low sperm count, or poor semen quality.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul>
<li>Hormonal imbalance</li>
<li>Thyroid imbalance</li>
<li>Polycystic ovary syndrome</li>
<li>Ovarian failure</li>
</ul>
