<p>Autism spectrum disorder (ASD) is common in children and affects social interaction, communication, and behavior. It is thought that ASD is caused by several complex genetic, and environmental factors or the use of vaccines.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Problems with social interaction</li><li>Poor eye contact</li><li>Problems with body gestures and language</li><li>Inability to understand emotions or feelings</li><li>Repetition of words or phrases</li><li>Anxiety or depression</li></ul>
