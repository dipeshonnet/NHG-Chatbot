<p>Eating disorders are characterized by an abnormal attitude towards food that affects behavior. Types of eating disorders may include anorexia nervosa, a condition associated with starvation or excessive exercise. Bulimia is a condition which is associated with binge eating followed by medication to evacuate the bowel or deliberately vomit. Binge eating disorder is a condition associated with overeating in a short space of time.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Missing meals</li><li>Worry about being fat</li><li>Repeated weighing</li><li>Eating low-calorie foods</li></ul>
