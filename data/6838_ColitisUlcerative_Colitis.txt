<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Ulcerative colitis (UC) is an autoimmune inflammatory condition affecting the colon and rectum. The lining of the colon becomes inflamed and ulcerated producing blood loss and pus.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Recurring diarrhea</li><li>Stool with blood, mucus, pus</li><li>Abdominal pain</li><li>Increased frequency of bowel emptying</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
