<p>White coating on the tongue is associated with oral thrush or a fungal infestation of the mouth. Oral candidiasis is caused by a group of yeasts called Candida.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>White patches or plaques in the mouth</li><li>Loss of taste or unpleasant taste</li><li>Redness inside the mouth and throat</li><li>burning sensation in the mouth</li></ul>
