<p>Indigestion or dyspepsia is a condition of pain or discomfort felt in the stomach leading to heartburn. When acid moves from the stomach into the esophagus, it creates a burning sensation behind the breastbone. Indigestion could be caused by a lack of stomach acid, acid reflux, medications, obesity, pregnancy, or a bad diet.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Bloated</li><li>Feeling sick</li><li>Belching</li><li>Flatulence</li><li>Regurgitating food or fluid from the stomach</li></ul>
