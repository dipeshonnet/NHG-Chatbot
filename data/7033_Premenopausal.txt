<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Premenopausal, perimenopause, or menopause is a transition in a woman when the ovaries gradually make less estrogen and stop releasing eggs. Symptoms of premenopausal may vary in intensity from heavy to no periods or varying frequencies of periods.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Hot flashes</li><li>Breast tenderness</li><li>Worse premenstrual syndrome</li><li>Lower sex drive</li><li>Fatigue</li><li>Irregular periods</li><li>Vaginal dryness</li><li>Discomfort during sex</li><li>Urine leakage when coughing or sneezing</li></ul>
