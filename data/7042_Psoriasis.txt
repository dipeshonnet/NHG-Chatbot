<p>Psoriasis is a skin condition that is presented as red, flaky, silvery crusty patches predominantly on elbows, knees, scalp, lower back, and ears. Psoriasis is a condition of increased production of skin cells, often genetic roots, affecting the immune system.  There are several types of psoriasis: plaque psoriasis is the most common form, scalp, nail, guttate, inverse, or pustular.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
