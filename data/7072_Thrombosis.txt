<p>Venous thromboembolism (VTE) is a serious potentially fatal medical condition where a blood clot is the major contributing factor. VTE may refer to deep vein thrombosis (DVT) where a blood clot lodges in a major vein, usually in the leg, or pulmonary embolism, where a blood clot affects blood flow from the heart to the lungs.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
