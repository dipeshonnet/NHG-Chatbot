<p>An allergy occurs when a person's immune system reacts to substances in the environment that are harmless to most<br>people. These substances are known as allergens and are found in house dust mites, pets, pollen, insects, molds, foods, and some medicines.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Fatigue and weakness</li><li>Headache</li><li>Light sensitivity</li><li>Poor memory</li><li>Difficult word finding</li><li>Difficulty concentration</li><li>Morning stiffness</li><li>Joint pain</li><li>Unusual skin sensations</li><li>Tingling and numbness</li><li>Shortness of breath</li><li>Sinus congestion or chronic cough</li></ul>
