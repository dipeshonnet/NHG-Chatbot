<p>Premenstrual syndrome (PMS) occurs in menstruating women , particularly in the luteal phase of their cycle. An imbalance of hormones is associated with PMS and associated mood changes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Bloating</li><li>Breast pain</li><li>Mood swings and irritability</li><li>Lack of libido</li><li>Pain in the abdominal area</li><li>Back pain</li><li>Headaches</li><li>Irregular bowel movement</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
