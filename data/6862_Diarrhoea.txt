<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Diarrhea can be caused as a result of dysentery, and food poisoning and is associated with gastroenteritis and gastrointestinal infections. Persistent diarrhea can lead to symptoms of dehydration which can be particularly dangerous, especially in the young.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Loose stool</li><li>Diarrhea and vomiting</li><li>Watery stool</li><li>Blood in the stool</li><li>Abdominal pain</li><li>Dehydration</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
