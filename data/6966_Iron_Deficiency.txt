<p>Iron deficiency anemia is a condition of a lack of iron required to store and carry oxygen in red blood cells. Other types of anemia can be caused by a lack of vitamin B12 or folate in the body.  Iron deficiency anemia may be caused by excessive bleeding in the stomach or intestine stomach and bowel cancer, or taking non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs.  In women of reproductive age, heavy periods and pregnancy are common causes of iron deficiency.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
