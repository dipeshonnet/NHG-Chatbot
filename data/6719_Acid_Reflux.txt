<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>At the entrance to your stomach is a valve, which is a ring of muscle called the lower esophageal sphincter (LES). Normally,<br>the LES closes as soon as food passes through it. If the LES doesn't close all the way or if it opens too often, acid<br>produced by your stomach can move up into your esophagus.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
