<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Asthma is a common long-term inflammatory disease of the airways of the lungs. It is characterized by variable and recurring symptoms, reversible air obstruction, and bronchospasm.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Shortness of breath</li><li>Chest tightness</li><li>Chest pain</li><li>Coughing or wheezing</li><li>Whistling or wheezing when exhaling</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
