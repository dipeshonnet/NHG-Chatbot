<p>Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) is a condition of the proliferation of ovarian cells and is associated with irregular periods, excess androgen hormones, insulin resistance, and an imbalance of female hormones. PCOS can present as irregular periods, amenorrhoea, hirsutism, hair loss, weight gain, or acne.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Irregular periods</li><li>Amenorrhoea</li><li>Difficulty getting pregnant</li><li>Excessive hair growth</li><li>Weight gain</li><li>Hair loss from the head</li><li>Oily skin or acne</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
