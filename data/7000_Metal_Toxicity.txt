<p>Heavy metal toxicity is becoming a serious medical health issue due to industrialization and exposure to heavy metals.  Metals that have known toxicity affecting many biochemical processes may include exposures to lead, mercury, cadmium, and arsenic. Exposures may occur through diet, medication,<br>environment or work.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
