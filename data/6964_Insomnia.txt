<p><!-- wp:paragraph -->Insomnia is difficulty getting to sleep or staying asleep long enough to feel refreshed upon awakening. Persistent insomnia can have a significant impact on quality of life which can affect energy, moods, stress, and relationships. The underlying causes of insomnia is associated with stress and anxiety, poor<br />sleeping environment, lifestyle factors, mental health issues, physical health conditions, and medication use.</p>
