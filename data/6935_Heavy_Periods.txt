<p>Heavy periods or menorrhagia is a condition of heavy bleeding during a menstrual cycle in a woman. Menorrhagia may be associated with PCOS, pelvic inflammatory disease, broids, endometriosis, underactive thyroid, or cancer in the womb.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Painful periods</li><li>Anemia</li><li>Heavy bleeding</li><li>Pelvic pain</li></ul>
