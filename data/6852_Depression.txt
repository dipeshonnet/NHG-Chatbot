<p>Depression is a neurological imbalance of neurotransmitters presenting as sadness, feeling down or feeling low. Depression affects people in different ways causing physical and mental symptoms. There are many types of depression, e.g., bipolar disorder, postnatal depression, and psychotic depression.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Low mood</li><li>Lack of enthusiasm</li><li>Altered appetite and sex drive</li><li>Constantly fatigued</li><li>Poor sleep</li><li>Aches and pains</li><li>Suicidal Ideation</li></ul>
