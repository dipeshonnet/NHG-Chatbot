<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Fibromyalgia is an autoimmune condition associated with pain all over the body.  Fibromyalgia is a complex condition that may be caused by genetic defects, imbalances in neurotransmitters, infections, chronic stress, or nutrient deficiencies.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Increased sensitivity to pain</li><li>Muscle stiffness</li><li>Difficulty sleeping</li><li>Extreme fatigue</li><li>Headaches</li><li>Mental health disturbances</li><li>Irritable bowel syndrome</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
