<p>Bipolar disorder, formerly known as manic depression, is a condition that affects your moods, which can swing from one extreme to another. People with bipolar disorder have periods or episodes of depression – feeling very low and lethargic mania – feeling very high and overactive (less severe mania is<br>known as hypomania).</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Feeling hopeless or sad</li><li>Irritability</li><li>Mental sluggishness</li><li>Fatigue</li><li>Inability to experience pleasure</li><li>Hallucinations</li><li>Exaggerated swings of mood</li><li>Poor cognition</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
