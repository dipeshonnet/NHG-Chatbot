<p>Acne is a skin condition characterized by red pimples on the skin, especially on the face, due to named or infected<br>sebaceous glands. We know that stress can aggravate acne, however, it may not be the absolute cause of it. Other<br>common causes of acne are hormone imbalances where an increase in hormone production occurs causing high levels of<br>androgens, the male sex hormones that include testosterone.  Testosterone signals the body to make more sebum, the oil<br>produced in the skin's oil glands.</p>
