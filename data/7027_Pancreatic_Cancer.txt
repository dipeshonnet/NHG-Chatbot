<p>Pancreatic cancer, one of the most serious forms of cancer, is associated with the abnormal growth of pancreatic cells. The rest notable signs of pancreatic cancer are a pain in the back or stomach, rapid unexpected weight loss, and jaundice.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Unexpected weight loss</li><li>Back or stomach pain</li><li>Jaundice</li><li>Nausea and vomiting</li><li>Indigestion</li></ul>
