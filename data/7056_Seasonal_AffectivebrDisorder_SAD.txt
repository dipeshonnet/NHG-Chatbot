<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD) is a type of depression that manifests in the dark winter months. A lack of sunlight affects the production of melatonin, and serotonin and disturbs circadian rhythms which can lead to symptoms of SAD.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Low mood</li><li>Irritability</li><li>Lethargy</li><li>Sleeping for longer periods of time</li><li>Carbohydrate cravings</li><li>Loss of interest in daily activities</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
