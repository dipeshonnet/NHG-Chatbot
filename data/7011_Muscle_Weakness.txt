<p>Muscle pain or myalgia refers to pain in the muscle tissue that may arise due to injury, overexertion, infections or inflammation. Muscle pain is the hallmark of chronic conditions like fibromyalgia and chronic fatigue syndrome.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Muscle pain</li><li>Tenderness</li><li>Muscle spasms</li><li>Stiffness and swelling</li></ul>
