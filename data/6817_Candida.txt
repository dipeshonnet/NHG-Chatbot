<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Oral thrush is a fungal infection of the mouth. It is not contagious and is usually successfully treated with antifungal medication. It is also called oral candidosis (or candidiasis) because it is caused by a group of yeasts called Candida.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Skin and nails infections</li><li>Fatigue</li><li>Digestive issues</li><li>Bloating</li><li>Constipation</li><li>Diarrhea</li><li>Journal infections</li><li>Sugar and refined carbohydrate cravings</li><li>Difficulty concentrating</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
