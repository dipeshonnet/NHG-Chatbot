<p>Anorexia Nervosa is a psychological illness with devastating physical consequences. It is characterized by low body weight and body image distortion with an obsessive fear of gaining weight which manifests itself by depriving the body of food. It often coincides with increased levels of exercise.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Obsessive count of calories</li><li>Excessive exercise</li><li>Miss meals</li><li>Eat very little</li><li>Promote vomiting</li><li>Abdominal pain</li><li>Fatigue</li><li>Feeling cold</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
