<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Helicobacter pylori is a bacterial infection of the digestive tract which attacks the stomach lining and is responsible for ulcers in the stomach and small intestine.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Peptic ulcers</li><li>Gastritis</li><li>Burping and belching</li><li>Bloating</li><li>Nausea</li><li>Vomiting</li><li>Loss of appetite or anorexia</li><li>Unexplained weight loss</li><li>Foul breath</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
