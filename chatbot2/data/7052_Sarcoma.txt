<p>Sarcomas or bone cancers can present as an Ewing, a rare form of bone cancer or soft tissue cancer. Kaposi sarcoma is cancer caused by a virus such as HIV. Soft tissue sarcoma affects tissues that connect to or surround other body structures or organs including tendons and ligaments. Each type of sarcoma has its own etiology, symptom, and treatment regime.</p>
