<p>Addiction is a primary, chronic disease of brain reward, motivation, memory, and related circuitry. Dysfunction in these<br>circuits leads to characteristic biological, psychological, social, and spiritual manifestations. This is reflected in an individual<br>pathologically pursuing reward and/or relief from substance use and other behaviors.</p>
