<p>Diabetes is an inflammatory condition caused by elevated blood sugar levels and the inability to produce enough insulin.  There are two types of diabetes: type I diabetes where the body’s immune system attacks and destroys cells that produce insulin, and type II diabetes where the body doesn’t produce enough insulin.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Feeling thirsty</li><li>Increased frequency of urination</li><li>Fatigue</li><li>Weight loss</li><li>Loss of muscle tissue</li><li>Cuts and wounds that heal slowly</li><li>Blurred vision</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
