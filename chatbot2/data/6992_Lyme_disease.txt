<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Lyme disease or Lyme Borreliosis is a bacterial infection spread by infected tics. Tics are spider-like creatures that feed on the blood of birds and mammals including humans.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>A circular rash at the site of tick bite</li><li>Flu-like symptoms</li><li>Fatigue</li><li>Chills</li><li>Headaches</li><li>Joint pain</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
