<p>Bowel cancer is a general term for cancer that begins in the large bowel. Depending on where cancer starts, bowel cancer is sometimes called colon or rectal cancer. Bowel cancer is one of the most common types of cancer diagnosed in the UK. Most people diagnosed are over the age of 60.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
