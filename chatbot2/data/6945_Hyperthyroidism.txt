<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Hyperthyroidism is a condition of an overactive thyroid with a thyroid gland producing too much thyroid hormone.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Nervousness</li><li>Anxiety</li><li>Irritability</li><li>Mood swings</li><li>difficulty sleeping</li><li>Sensitivity to heat</li><li>Swelling in the neck area</li><li>Twitching or trembling</li><li>Palpitations</li><li>Weight loss</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
