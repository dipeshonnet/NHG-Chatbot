<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Irritable bowel syndrome is a common long-term condition of the gastrointestinal tract. It causes bouts of abdominal pain, bloating, diarrhea or constipation. The prevalence of IBS is increasing possibly due to an improper diet or stress.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Abdominal pain</li><li>Bloating</li><li>Changes in bowel habits</li><li>Excessive wind</li><li>Mucus in the bowel</li><li>Bowel urgency</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
