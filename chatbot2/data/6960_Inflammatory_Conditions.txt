<p>Inflammation is a self-protection mechanism within the body that aims to remove harmful stimuli, irritants, or pathogens to stimulate a healing process.  Inflammation may be associated with pain, swelling or supurration. Acute inflammation starts rapidly and becomes severe, e.g. bronchitis, u or meningitis.  Chronic inflammation is usually long-term resulting from an autoimmune response to a self-antigen where the immune system attacks healthy tissue. e.g. asthma, rheumatoid arthritis, peptic ulcer, sinusitis.</p>
