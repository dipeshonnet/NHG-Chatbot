<p>Growth disorders are a broad condition associated with growing pains, hypermobility, or dwarfism. Growing pains are aches and pains cooing mainly in large muscles like leg muscles in growing children. Dwarfism characterized by short stature may be associated with lack of growth or limbs being shorter or out<br>of proportion with other body parts.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
