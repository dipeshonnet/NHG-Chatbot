<p>Benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) is common in the aging man and is associated with prostate enlargement, prostatitis or<br>inflammation of the prostate, or prostate cancer. The prostate gland surrounds the urethra which ordinarily produces a thick<br>white fluid mixed with sperm to create semen.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
