<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Rheumatoid arthritis (RA) is a long-term inflammatory condition affecting joints. RA is an autoimmune disease affecting the immune system and may lead to or increase the risk of complications such as carpal tunnel syndrome, inflammation of other areas of the body, or heart attacks.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Joint pain</li><li>Swelling and stiffness</li><li>Inflammation and redness of joints</li><li>Dry eyes</li><li>Fever</li><li>Chest pain</li><li>Fatigue</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
