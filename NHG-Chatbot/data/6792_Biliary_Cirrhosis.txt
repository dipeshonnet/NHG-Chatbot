<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Cirrhosis is scarring of the liver caused by continuous long-term liver damage. Scar tissue replaces healthy tissue in the liver<br>and prevents the liver from working properly.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Associated Symptoms</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Nausea</li><li>vomiting</li><li>Pain under the right rib cage</li><li>Yellowing of skin</li></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
