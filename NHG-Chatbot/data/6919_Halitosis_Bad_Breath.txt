<p>Halitosis or bad breath may be caused by poor oral hygiene, the build-up of bacteria or toxins in the mouth, gum disease,<br>smoking, or drinking alcohol. The underlying cause may need to be established, e.g. infections in the gut or liver toxicity.</p>
