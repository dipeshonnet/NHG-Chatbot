<p>Prostate cancer affects the aging male and is associated with enlargement of the prostate gland (benign prostatic hyperplasia) and may be asymptomatic. Prostate cancer progresses slowly and can affect erectile function, urine flow, and moods.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
