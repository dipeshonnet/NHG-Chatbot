<p>Adrenal Fatigue is a decrease in the adrenal glands' ability to carry out this normal function. It is commonly caused by<br>chronic stress from any source (including emotional, physical, mental, or environmental) that exceeds the body's capacity<br>to adjust appropriately to the demands placed on it by the stress.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
